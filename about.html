LEFN-About Us

Company Profile Ningbo Lefeng New Energy Co., Ltd. was established in 2017 with a registered capital of 20 million yuan and total assets of 173 million yuan. It is located in Binhai Industrial Park, Xiangshan County. It covers an area of ​​approximately 63,000 square meters. It is a new energy company integrating R&D and manufacturing of photovoltaic products, investment, construction, operation and maintenance of photovoltaic power stations.

The company is committed to the manufacture of photovoltaic products and the development and construction of photovoltaic power stations, promote the application of renewable energy, and create a green and livable environment for human beings. In the past 3 years, Lefeng’s cumulative shipments reached 2GW , with products covering 23 countries and regions. At present, the company has 3 automated production lines with a module production capacity of 700MW .
